Why Nepal?

Since I played Uncharted 2 Among Thieves, the idea of travelling to Nepal has been on my head. I really like that game and I want to travel to the same places where the gameplay developed.

Of course I know a game is not like reality, but I still think that travelling to Nepal could be a worth-living experience.

Not only Nepal

Nepal is not gonna be the only place to visit on this trip. As well I will visit cities like New Delhi, India and Lhasa, Tibet in China. As well other Himalayan cities.
